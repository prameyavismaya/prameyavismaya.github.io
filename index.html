About

These lectures and notes are born from a belief that mathematical beauty should be accessible to all.

Each course is designed to take you from first principles to deep understanding. The video lectures build intuition through carefully chosen examples and visual arguments, while the companion notes provide the rigorous foundations — complete with proofs, exercises, and the occasional detour into mathematical curiosities.

"Mathematics is not about numbers, equations, computations, or algorithms: it is about understanding."

— William Paul Thurston

Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a self-learner exploring new territory, or simply someone who finds joy in the elegance of a well-crafted proof — these materials are for you.
